I'm Pratyush — a 2nd year CS student at IIIT Hyderabad who somehow ended up on five design teams simultaneously. I'm not sure how that happened but I've chosen to lean in.

I work across game development (Unity, Unreal, Godot), systems programming (C, C++), and web dev. My GitHub is chirrupings123. My Instagram is @_chirrupings_. My sleep schedule is undefined.
